MRIS-B STUDENT COUNCIL
What Is The Student Council
The Student Council is a student-led organization dedicated to representing the voices of the student body. It serves as a bridge between students and the school administration, ensuring that students' opinions and ideas are heard and considered in school decisions
Our Vision
Contributing to providing various programs and activities, discussing student-related problems, and trying to find solutions consistent with national values ​​and principles
Our Mission
To provide a productive environment that provides a space for the student to express himself and his abilities through an integrated educational, cultural and entertaining community under the principle of cooperation and excellence
